My Transition into Tech

It's been quite a journey for me –- one that started on the athletic field and ultimately led to a passion for software engineering. While the path may seem unconventional, the skills and experiences I gained along the way have proven invaluable in my tech career.

From Athlete to Coach: Building a Foundation

My early career was deeply rooted in athletics, first as an athlete and then as a track and field coach specializing in jumps. This experience instilled in me the importance of discipline, leadership, and teamwork –- qualities that are highly relevant in the collaborative world of the software development cycle.

A New Direction: Logistics and the Spark of Optimization

Seeking a career with more long-term stability, I transitioned to a Warehouse Logistics Specialist role at Milestone Technologies. This experience exposed me to the critical role of precision and efficiency in operations management, sparking my interest in leveraging technology for process optimization.

Discovering the Power of Code

To streamline my work in logistics, I began exploring Python scripting to automate repetitive tasks. This not only improved efficiency but also allowed me to focus on more strategic initiatives, such as leading and mentoring my team. This hands-on experience ignited a passion for software development and its problem-solving potential.

Rising to the Challenge: Quality Assurance and Automation

My initiative in automation led to a promotion to Junior Quality Assurance Analyst at Milestone Technologies. In this role, I gained a deeper understanding of the software development lifecycle, collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure product quality. I leveraged tools like PyTest and Selenium to automate testing processes, significantly enhancing efficiency and product reliability.

The Pandemic Pivot: Embracing My Passion

When the COVID-19 pandemic forced our office to close, I found myself at a crossroads. This period of uncertainty clarified my true calling: software engineering. Determined to pursue this path, I enrolled at De Anza College to formally study Computer Science. The challenges of the rigorous coursework only solidified my commitment and resilience.

Reaching New Heights: Academic Excellence and Beyond

My dedication to my studies paid off. I excelled at De Anza, earning a place on the Dean's List and thus an acceptance into the competitive computer science program at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. This achievement cemented my path to becoming a software engineer.

The Journey Continues

Now, as a Computer Science student at Cal Poly SLO, I'm eager to contribute to innovative projects and collaborate with talented peers. My diverse background allows me to approach challenges with a unique perspective. I'm excited to see where this journey leads in the dynamic world of technology.
